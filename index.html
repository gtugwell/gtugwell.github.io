About Me

I started off my college career aiming to be a chemical engineer, however I soon realized I was completely ignoring my love for technology. Every day after my chemistry classes, I would go back and read, watch, or experiment with something involving computers or coding. I knew I would regret sticking to my original path, so I decided to take a leap and go into computer science, and I'm so glad I took that path instead!

Some of the things that enjoy outside of computer science including taking photos, working out, and eating with my friends. Over the years I have organized small running groups, and more recently workout groups, and some of our current goals are to run in the 2022 Houston Marathon.
